# Cost Per 100 Miles Calculator

## Purpose

Because I drive an EV, people often ask me what it costs to run, compared with a gas car. I find myself redoing the same calculations each time. One useful way to think about this (outside of important maintenance factors): how much does it cost to drive 100 miles? 

This script is designed to calculate the cost of driving 100 miles in an EV at different efficiency levels and different electricity prices -- and to calculate the cost of driving 100 miles in a gas car at different fuel-efficiency levels and different gasoline prices.
